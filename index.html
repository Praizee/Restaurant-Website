About Us

Welcome to Lé Classique, where our passion for food meets our commitment to excellence. Established in 1985, we are a family-owned and operated restaurant that takes pride in serving delicious and authentic Italian cuisine.

  • Our menu features a wide variety of dishes, each prepared with only the freshest and highest quality ingredients.
  • Our team of skilled chefs and friendly staff are dedicated to ensuring that every visit to Lé Classique is a memorable one.
  • We also offer private dining rooms for special occasions or business meetings, as well as a full bar with a wide selection of cocktails and wine.

Thank you for choosing Lé Classique, and we look forward to serving you!

Seafood Paella:

Experience the taste of Spain with our seafood paella, featuring a medley of fresh seafood, saffron-infused rice, and aromatic spices.

Our seafood paella is a classic Spanish dish that is as delicious as it is beautiful. This dish is made with a flavorful combination of fresh seafood, such as shrimp, mussels, and calamari, mixed with saffron-infused rice and a variety of aromatic spices. The result is a colorful and aromatic dish that will transport you straight to the Mediterranean coast.

Butternut Squash Ravioli:

Indulge in the warm and comforting flavors of our butternut squash ravioli, served with a sage-butter sauce.

Our butternut squash ravioli is a vegetarian delight, made with freshly made pasta stuffed with a creamy and flavorful butternut squash filling. The ravioli is then topped with a sage-butter sauce that adds a rich and comforting flavor to the dish. This special is perfect for those looking for a warm and hearty meal that is both delicious and satisfying.

Mediterranean Grilled Chicken:

Travel to the sunny shores of the Mediterranean with our flavorful grilled chicken, served with a refreshing cucumber-tomato salad.

Our Mediterranean grilled chicken is a light and refreshing dish that will transport you straight to the beaches of Greece. This dish is made with tender chicken marinated in a blend of Mediterranean herbs and spices, then grilled to perfection. The chicken is served with a refreshing cucumber-tomato salad that adds a bright and tangy flavor to the dish.

Prime Rib:

Our Prime Rib special is a steakhouse classic, featuring a juicy and flavorful cut of slow-roasted prime rib, served with a side of au jus and horseradish sauce. This dish is cooked to perfection and served with your choice of side dishes for a complete and satisfying meal.
